Asia Tech Summit 2024

Participated as a delegate in the Asia Tech Summit held in Bangkok, Thailand. Engaged with industry leaders and explored cutting-edge technologies in AI, cloud computing, and digital innovation across the Asian tech ecosystem.

International Exchange Program

Selected as a scholar for the Southeast Asia exchange program, spending time at partner universities across multiple countries, fostering cross-cultural technical collaboration and expanding professional networks.

National Science Fair - First Prize

Won first prize at the National Science Fair for an innovative machine learning project demonstrating real-time environmental monitoring and predictive analytics. The project showcased applications of AI in sustainability and climate research.

IoT Research Excellence Recognition

Recognized for excellence in IoT innovation through a comprehensive smart city monitoring system. Developed IoT sensors and backend analytics to track urban air quality, traffic patterns, and infrastructure health in real-time.

Best Data Science Project - Predictive Analytics

Awarded for developing an advanced predictive analytics model using deep learning techniques. The project demonstrated expertise in data processing, feature engineering, and model optimization for real-world business applications.

University Cricket Team Captain

Appointed as captain of the university cricket team, leading the squad through multiple seasons with strategic gameplay and mentorship. Demonstrated leadership, teamwork, and competitive excellence on the field.

Regional Badminton Championship - Runner-up

Achieved runner-up position in the regional badminton championship, demonstrating athletic excellence and strategic gameplay. Successfully represented the university at competitive regional tournaments.

National Photography Exhibition - Featured Photographer

Selected as a featured photographer in the National Photography Exhibition, showcasing landscape and urban photography series documenting the transformation of Bangladeshi cities. Received recognition for artistic vision and technical excellence.

Street Photography Award - Best Urban Storytelling

Awarded for excellence in street photography, capturing authentic moments that tell stories of urban life and cultural diversity. Recognized for technical skill and compelling visual narratives.

Coding Bootcamp Leader - Community Service

Led multiple coding bootcamp sessions for underprivileged youth, teaching fundamental programming concepts, problem-solving, and industry-relevant skills. Impacted over 200 students in rural communities.

Digital Literacy Champion - Rural Tech Education

Recognized as a champion in the Rural Tech Education Program, organizing and conducting workshops that brought digital literacy and coding education to remote areas, bridging the technology gap.

Programming Contest Winner - Algorithm Challenge 2024

Winner of the Algorithm Challenge programming contest, solving complex computational problems efficiently. Demonstrated strong algorithmic thinking and coding proficiency competing against top programmers.

Hackathon Finalist - Top 10 Innovation Sprint

Made it to the top 10 finalists in the Innovation Sprint Hackathon, developing an innovative solution combining AI and web technologies. Worked in agile teams under time pressure demonstrating rapid prototyping skills.
